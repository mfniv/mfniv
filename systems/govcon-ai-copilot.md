## Summary

Your team’s always-on regulatory knowledge base — powered by AI, grounded in source documents.

### The Challenge

Government contractors operate under thousands of pages of regulatory guidance — FAR, DFARS, CMMC, NIST 800-171, SBA rules, and agency-specific policies. Finding the right clause, interpreting applicability, or verifying a compliance requirement means hours lost searching PDFs and cross-referencing supplements. Smaller firms feel this pain most acutely: they lack dedicated compliance departments, yet face the same regulatory burden as primes.

### What GovCon AI Co-pilot Does

GovCon AI Co-pilot is a retrieval-augmented AI assistant that lets your team ask plain-English questions and get grounded, source-cited answers drawn directly from federal acquisition regulations, cybersecurity frameworks, and your organization’s own policies. It doesn’t guess. Every response traces back to the authoritative document, section, and clause.

- **Regulatory Knowledge Base:** Ask questions in natural language about FAR, DFARS, CMMC, NIST 800-171/53, SBA regulations, and more. Answers cite specific parts, subparts, and clauses — no hallucination, no guesswork.
- **Persona-Scoped Retrieval:** Five built-in personas — from Acquisition Officer to CMMC Compliance Manager — automatically filter the corpus to the regulations most relevant to each role, reducing noise and accelerating answers.
- **Proposal & Capture Support:** Accelerates compliance matrix generation, RFP requirement parsing, and executive summary drafting. Maps solicitation requirements against your capability library and flags gaps before submission.

### On the Roadmap
